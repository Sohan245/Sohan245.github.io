01 / About

Background

I'm a cybersecurity graduate student at the University of Houston (GPA 4.0), expected to complete my M.S. in Cybersecurity in May 2026. My work spans the full offensive security lifecycle — from penetration testing and vulnerability assessment to building novel security tooling.

My research has been accepted at BlackHat Arsenal USA & Europe and published at IEEE FMLDS, focusing on AI-driven exploit generation, LLM-powered initial access, and cryptographic analysis frameworks.

I currently serve as a Teaching Assistant at UH and have hands-on experience in IT/OT security, having interned at KPMG, Third Coast Terminals, and REINFOSEC — where I designed the Hardware Vulnerability Scoring System (HVSS) for IoT devices.
